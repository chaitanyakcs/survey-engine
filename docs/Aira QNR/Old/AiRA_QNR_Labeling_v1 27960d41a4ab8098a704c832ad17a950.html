Steps:

  1. All Golden Data QNR’s are labelled with the list mentioned below at the Question level, Section level and Overall QNR level.
  1. Some labels have a Mandatory flag as “Yes”. This indicated that a question with this label is required in the final questionnaire
  1. Some labels have “Applicable Labels” against them, this indicates that QNRs with those labels should also have this label.
